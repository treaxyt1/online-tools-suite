Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

🔒 OnlineToolFree is privacy-first. All tools run locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or share your data.

1. Overview

OnlineToolFree ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at onlinetoolfree.com (the "Service").

The short version: We don't collect your personal data. All our tools run entirely in your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike most websites, OnlineToolFree is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy:

  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No File Uploads: Files you process with our tools are never uploaded to our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.
  • No Tracking: We don't use tracking cookies, pixels, or fingerprinting techniques.
  • No Account Required: You can use all tools without creating an account or signing up.

3. How Our Tools Work

All OnlineToolFree tools are client-side applications. This means:

  • Your data is processed directly in your web browser using JavaScript.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • When you close your browser tab, all data is automatically cleared.

4. Analytics

We may use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use our website. These analytics are:

  • Aggregated and anonymous
  • Do not track individual users
  • Do not use cookies
  • Do not collect personal information

5. Cookies

OnlineToolFree uses only essential cookies for basic website functionality:

  • Theme Preference: To remember your light/dark mode preference.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party cookies.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: To load web fonts. Google may collect anonymous usage data.
  • Cloudflare: For content delivery and security. Cloudflare may collect IP addresses for security purposes.

7. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's no risk of data breaches affecting your personal information. Your data remains entirely within your browser and under your control.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is available to users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, we don't knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data, there's no data to access, correct, or delete. You have complete control over your data at all times.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

  • Website: Contact Page

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with GDPR requirements. Since we don't collect personal data, most GDPR provisions don't apply. However, we ensure:

  • Transparency about our data practices
  • No data processing without consent
  • No third-party data sharing

13. CCPA Compliance

For California residents, we comply with CCPA requirements. We do not sell personal information because we do not collect personal information.
