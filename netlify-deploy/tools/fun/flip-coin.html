About Coin Flip

A coin flip is a simple way to make a binary decision. Each flip has a 50/50 chance of landing on heads or tails, making it a fair and unbiased method for settling disputes or making choices.

Common uses:

  • Making quick decisions
  • Starting sports games
  • Settling friendly disputes
  • Random selection between two options
