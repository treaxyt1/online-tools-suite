Frequently Asked Questions

What is gross vs net salary?

Gross salary is your total earnings before any deductions. Net salary (take-home pay) is what you receive after taxes and other deductions.

What is FICA tax?

FICA (Federal Insurance Contributions Act) is 7.65% of your salary, consisting of 6.2% for Social Security and 1.45% for Medicare.

How accurate is this calculator?

This calculator provides estimates using standard tax brackets and deductions. For precise calculations, consult a tax professional or use official IRS tools.
