How to Use the Tip Calculator

  1. Enter your bill amount
  2. Select a tip percentage (15%, 18%, 20%, 25%) or enter a custom amount
  3. Enter the number of people splitting the bill
  4. View the tip amount, total bill, and amount per person

Tipping Guidelines: In the United States, standard tipping ranges from 15-20% for good service, 20-25% for excellent service. Adjust based on your location and service quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good tip percentage?

A standard tip is 15-20% for good service. For excellent service, consider 20-25%. For poor service, 10-15% is acceptable.

Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?

Traditionally, tips are calculated on the pre-tax amount. However, many people tip on the total including tax for simplicity.

How do I split a bill evenly?

Enter the total bill amount and the number of people. The calculator will automatically show the amount each person should pay, including their share of the tip.
