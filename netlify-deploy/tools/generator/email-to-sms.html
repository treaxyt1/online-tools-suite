How it works

Most mobile carriers provide an "Email to SMS" gateway. This allows you to send an email to a specific address, and the carrier will convert it into a text message delivered to the recipient's phone.

Format: [Phone Number]@[Carrier Gateway]

Why use this?

  • Send texts from your computer without a phone.
  • Automate SMS notifications from servers/scripts.
  • Backup method when cellular service is poor but WiFi works.
