About RGB and CMYK

RGB (Red, Green, Blue) is an additive color model used for digital screens. CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Key/Black) is a subtractive color model used in printing.

Converting between these two is essential when preparing digital designs for physical printing. Note that because RGB has a wider color gamut than CMYK, some bright/neon colors on screen may appear duller in print.
