About Dice Roller

Roll virtual dice online for board games, tabletop RPGs, or any situation where you need random numbers. Choose from standard 6-sided dice or polyhedral dice used in games like Dungeons & Dragons.

Dice Types:

  • D4: 4-sided die (tetrahedron)
  • D6: Standard 6-sided cube die
  • D8: 8-sided die (octahedron)
  • D10: 10-sided die (pentagonal trapezohedron)
  • D12: 12-sided die (dodecahedron)
  • D20: 20-sided die (icosahedron) - popular in D&D
  • D100: 100-sided die (percentile)
